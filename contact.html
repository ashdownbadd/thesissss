Emergency Hotlines

EMERGENCY HOTLINE

For any kind of emergency, the most important number to remember is the Philippine National Emergency Hotline.

Emergency Hotline: 911

PNP

In case of crime or any law enforcement needs, contact the Philippine National Police.

Emergency Hotline: 9-117 / 8723-0401 / 8537-4500

BFP

If you encounter a fire emergency, immediately call the Bureau of Fire Protection.

Emergency Hotline: (02) 8426-0219 / (02) 8790-8400

NDRRMC

For natural disasters and calamities, reach out to NDRRMC.

Emergency Hotline: (02) 8421-1918 / (02) 8913-2786

DOH

For health-related emergencies, contact the Department of Health.

Emergency Hotline: 8711-1001

NCMH

If you or someone you know is in crisis, reach out to this hotline for help.

Emergency Hotline: 0966-351-4518
