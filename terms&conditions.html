Terms and Conditions


Welcome to the Student Location Tracking System! By using this system, you agree to comply with the following terms and conditions. Please read them carefully.


1. Introduction

This Student Location Tracking System is designed to help administrators track the location of students within the campus premises. By registering and using this system, you consent to the collection and use of your location data as described in these Terms and Conditions.

2. Collection of Location Data

Upon registration, the system will request permission to access your real-time location. This data will only be used to monitor whether students are within the campus boundaries and for administrative purposes. The system will not track students' locations outside of the campus, and location data will not be shared with third parties unless required by law.

3. Student Responsibilities

As a user of the Student Location Tracking System, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate personal and location information upon registration.
  • Keep your login credentials confidential and secure.
  • Immediately report any unauthorized use of your account or suspected breach of security.

4. Use of Location Data

The data collected by the system will be used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Tracking student presence within campus boundaries.
  • Ensuring student safety and attendance monitoring.
  • Providing data to school authorities for campus security management.

Your data will not be used for any commercial purposes or shared with any external parties.

5. Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. All collected personal and location information will be stored securely and will only be accessible by authorized school administrators.
